Conquer Kingdoms

About Conquer Kingdoms

Conquer Kingdoms is a 3D strategy adventure where you play as a fearless king leading five trusty soldiers. Explore lands, collect treasures, and battle enemies to build your kingdom.

How to Play

Build your empire and show your rivals who's in charge! Guide your soldiers through unknown territory to battle foes and collect treasure chests.

Controls

  • Move Troops: WASD/arrows or drag mouse
  • Special Attack: Spacebar
  • Zoom: Mouse wheel
